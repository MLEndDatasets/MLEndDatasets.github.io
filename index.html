Welcome to MLEnd Datasets!

MLEnd Datasets are collection of datasets, acquired during the course - Principle of Machine Learning at Queen Mary Univeristy of London. During the course, students are involved the process of data collection and at the end, students work on these datasets for mini-projects. Participating in data collection process gives students a unique exposure, which includes a deployment-first perspective of Machine Learning. Students learns to embrace the noisy and messy datasets.
